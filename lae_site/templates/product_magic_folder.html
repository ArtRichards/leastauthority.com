How it will work

Just put files (and folders full of files) inside the Magic Folder, and they will appear on any other authorized computer with Magic Folder installed. When you edit a file inside Magic Folder, it syncs automatically.

No more emailing files to yourself, no more clunky upload-download services, no more flash drives!
