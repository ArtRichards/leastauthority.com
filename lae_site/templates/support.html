Product Support

The quickest way to receive product support is via IRC. The project developers can be found on irc.freenode.net in the channel #leastauthorityenterprises. There, you can find support for product setup, sign up, and technical inquiries.

For general product support questions, product inquiries, billing, or general questions, please send a message to info@leastauthority.com with the subject "Product Support" and you will be contacted as soon as possible.

To provide greater transparency into the reliability of our servers, our monitoring system is open to public access by following this link, and logging with username "guest" and password "guest". (this currently gives a certificate warning, which will be fixed soon) Once logged in you can view performance graphs for user EC2 servers by clicking on an IP address, and then clicking the "Graphs" button in the left-hand menu. We also have a public mailing list for monitoring alerts. In both of these, servers are identified by their public IP address. New customers are informed of their server's public IP address in the sign-up confirmation email, and existing customers will be given this information shortly.
