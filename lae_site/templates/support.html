Product Support

The quickest way to receive product support is via IRC. The project developers can be found on irc.freenode.net in the channel #leastauthorityenterprises. There, you can find support for product setup, sign up, and technical inquiries.

For general product support questions, product inquiries, billing, or general questions, please send a message to info@leastauthority.com with the subject "Product Support" and you will be contacted as soon as possible.

Transparency

To provide greater transparency into the reliability of our servers, our monitoring system is open to public access by following this link, and logging in with username "guest" and password "guest". Once logged in you can view performance graphs for customer servers by clicking on an IP address, and then clicking the "Graphs" button in the left-hand menu. We also have a public mailing list for monitoring alerts. In both of these, servers are identified by their public IP address. New customers are informed of their server's public IP address in the sign-up confirmation email, and existing customers will be given this information shortly.

The Python source code for this website, our automation and monitoring scripts, and our fork of Tahoe-LAFS to support Amazon S3 are all open-sourced under the same licenses as Tahoe-LAFS itself. The S3 backend depends on a fork of the txaws library that is open-sourced under the MIT / X / Expat License.

To obtain this source code, install darcs and then use:

  darcs get --lazy https://leastauthority.com/static/source/ticket999-S3-backend
  darcs get --lazy https://leastauthority.com/static/source/leastauthority.com
  darcs get --lazy https://leastauthority.com/static/source/monitors

The patches to txaws are at <https://leastauthority.com/static/patches>.
