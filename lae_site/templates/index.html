What makes us different?


100% client-side encryption

Unlike all the widely-used, PRISM-compatible services out there, S4 does client-side encryption on every file. The server doesn't have the ability to spy on or untraceably modify your files even if it tries!

Open source transparency

Unlike other client-side-encryption services like Tarsnap and SpiderOak, S4 is free, open source software. Anyone can read the code and confirm that it doesn't have secret backdoors. Unlike users of Skype or Hushmail, you don't have to take our word for it that your backups are secure.
